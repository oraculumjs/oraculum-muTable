¶ Libs

By packing all of our libs into the Oraculum container, we can skirt around the nuisance of requiring each of them explicitly in our define[] blocks and simply request a reference to the library from Oraculum. It lends itself well to more readable code.

 

  Oraculum.define 'interact', (-> interact), singleton: true
